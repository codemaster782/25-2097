Seasonal Menu & Artisanal Wine Pairings

Our kitchen embraces farm‑to‑table philosophy, crafting classic coq au vin, bouillabaisse, and crème brûlée using organic ingredients sourced from local producers every morning before service.

We invite you to enjoy an intimate dining experience with soft candlelight, hand‑selected wines, and live piano melodies every Friday and Saturday evening from 7 PM.

Reservations are highly recommended for dinner service; walk‑ins are welcome based on availability, and private dining options exist for celebrations up to 24 guests.
