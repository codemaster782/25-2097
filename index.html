📖 About CodeShoes

Founded in 2023, CodeShoes originated from a passion to merge software engineering with high-end footwear. Our team of engineers, designers, and biomechanics experts create sneakers that adapt to your movement.

We believe in sustainable tech: 40% less carbon footprint per shoe, and fully recyclable packaging. Every pair includes open-source firmware for developers to customize LED patterns and haptic feedback.
