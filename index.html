Founded in 2020, Solelace emerged from a collective of sneaker designers and material engineers. We fuse modern aesthetics with biomechanical comfort — because every step matters.

Our workshop in Portland, OR, crafts small-batch releases with premium leathers, recycled rubber, and water-based adhesives.
